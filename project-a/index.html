Appearance:

Dop looks like a small bright yellow tennis ball with soft pink cheeks, blinking eyes, and thin wavy arms and legs. His body slightly shakes when excited.

Key Characteristics:

  • Expresses emotion through oscillation and shaking
  • Blinks naturally over time
  • Feels safe only on a tennis racketReacts through subtle
  • Arms and legs move in wave-like motion

What we know so far about Dop🎾 ? Dop🎾 is a small emotional tennis ball creature who expresses feelings through motion. Fast oscillation shows excitement, slow movement shows calmness. He feels safest on a tennis racket and dreams of flying. His behavior changes depending on interaction and environment.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Users interact with Dop by moving the mouse, which controls the tennis racket — his safe space. Dop loves when someone plays with him. When the racket comes close, he becomes happy and excited, bouncing energetically around the court. When no one plays with him and the racket moves away, Dop becomes sad and his mood changes. This interaction shows that Dop enjoys attention and feels happiest when someone spends time playing with him.
